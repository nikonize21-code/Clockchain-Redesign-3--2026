01

Your entry was hashed and submitted

The text you typed is sent to the logging endpoint as a small JSON payload and fingerprinted into a content hash.

02

It was anchored in the current block

Clockchain signs a new block every second. Your entry lands in the block for that exact moment, fixing it in time.

03

You got back a verifiable timestamp

The response includes the UTC timestamp, block height, and an on-chain receipt — anyone can independently verify it later.
